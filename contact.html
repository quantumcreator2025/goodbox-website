Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our operations and website.

How are the operations of The Good Box Project funded?
Our operations are funded entirely through individual donations, corporate CSR partnerships, and community fundraising events. We maintain 100% financial transparency and publish annual reports on our website.
How can I track the status of my donation on the website?
Currently, we send a detailed receipt and impact report via email within 7 days of receiving a donation. We are working on a donor portal that will allow you to track your impact in real-time directly on the website.
Are the stories and photos on the website real?
Yes, absolute authenticity is our core value. Every story shared on our website and social media is from a real beneficiary or volunteer. We take consent before sharing any personal details.
Can I volunteer at your Mumbai operations center?
Yes! Our main operations center is in Mumbai. You can visit us for ration packing sessions every Saturday. Please fill out the "Collaborate" form to schedule a visit so we can prepare for your arrival.
Is my data safe on this website?
We respect your privacy. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Your information is used solely for communication regarding your donation or volunteering interest.
